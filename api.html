Discover your passion, connect with others, and make a difference.

Why Join Korus ?

  • Networking:Connect with fellow musicians and expand your social circle.
  • Skill Development:Improve your musical abilities through practice and collaboration
  • Performance Opportunities:Gain stage experience and showcase your talent.
  • Creative Collaboration:Work with others to create original music and projects.
  • Cultural Exposure:Discover new genres, instruments, and global musical traditions.

What We Do in Korus

  • Vocal Workshops: Learn from professional vocal coaches to enhance skills.
  • Musical Exploration: Experiment with various genres, from classical to contemporary.
  • Recording Sessions: Record and produce songs as a group.
  • Performances: Opportunities to sing at concerts, events, and competitions.

How to Join Korus

  • Recruitment Drive: Apply in the Recruitment form released by society and fill all the reqiured sections
  • Audition:Offline mode with time limit of 2min (your song)
  • Interview: Personality , Character and some other Questions
